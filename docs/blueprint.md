# **App Name**: Dra Pami

## Core Features:

- Hero Section & Call-to-Action: Engaging display with the Dra. Pami's professional image, impactful tagline, and a prominent 'Agendar Cita vía WhatsApp' button for immediate booking.
- Personal Brand Story: A dedicated section showcasing Dra. Pami's professional journey, qualifications, and compassionate approach to patient care, building trust and connection.
- Treatments and Innovations Grid: An interactive grid detailing dental services (Orthodontics, Esthetics, Cleaning), color-coded for clarity and briefly explaining innovative methodologies.
- AI-Powered Dental Tips Tool: An interactive tool offering personalized dental hygiene advice or answering common patient queries based on simple user inputs, complementing the educational content.
- Patient Education Carousel: A dynamic carousel displaying practical dental health tips and articles like 'Técnicas de cepillado correctas' and 'Cuidado de brackets' for patient empowerment.
- Clinic Locations Visualizer: A clear, visual list of all clinic locations where Dra. Pami attends, including integrated map views or professional photos of the consultories.
- Quick Contact & Floating WhatsApp: An easily accessible contact form for inquiries and a persistent floating button for direct WhatsApp communication.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A bright and professional blue (#5AA1D8) for prominent calls to action and section headers, signifying trust and reliability. This corresponds to a hue for clean, professional health services.
- Background Colors: A clean pure white (#FFFFFF) paired with a very light, almost imperceptible blue-gray (#F0F4F2) for spacious, modern sections, promoting a sense of cleanliness and calm.
- Accent Color: A refreshing turquoise (#5CC0C4) used for subheadings and interactive elements, analogous to the primary for visual harmony, while adding a friendly touch.
- Complementary Palette: Soft purple (#BB6AA8), gentle orange (#F9B35D), and delicate pink (#F298C0) are reserved for distinguishing treatment categories, icons, and special highlights, providing warmth and approachability.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) will be used throughout the site, offering a modern, legible, and professional aesthetic suitable for both impactful titles and extensive patient education content, reinforcing the clean and minimal design.
- Icon Style: Utilize modern, minimalist, and outline-based icons for services, interactive elements, and navigation to maintain a clean aesthetic. Icons for treatments will leverage the complementary color palette to differentiate categories.
- Page Structure: Implement a spacious, modular layout with clear separation between sections, utilizing generous white space and light grey backgrounds to guide the user's eye and enhance readability. Content is presented in easily digestible blocks like grids and carousels.
- Subtle Animations: Implement gentle transitions and hover effects on interactive elements such as buttons, treatment cards, and the patient education carousel. These animations will be fluid and non-distracting, enhancing the user experience without sacrificing professionalism.
